The Work

In "The Speaking Wall," words emerge from stone itself—an impossible dialogue between the permanent and the ephemeral.
This piece explores how silence can become a language more powerful than speech.

Visual Language

Letters carved in real-time, each character a small violence against stillness. The typography breathes, shifts, reforms—never settling into meaning.

Sound Design

The absence of sound becomes presence. Only the faintest whisper of stone moving, almost below perception.

Emotional Core

That moment when you have everything to say but no words come. The wall speaks what we cannot.

Technical Approach

Filmed over 48 hours using time-lapse and real-time capture. Each frame manually adjusted to create the breathing effect.
