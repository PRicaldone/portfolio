The Work

A concrete wall, isolated in darkness. An invisible gesture carves it into the surface, letter by letter.
No emphasis, no spectacle — only friction. The gesture is neither impulsive nor theatrical. It is necessary.

Visual Language

A concrete wall, dark space, controlled light. Letters appear one by one — carved, not written. The image is still. The action is slow and irreversible.

Sound Design

Silence is not absence, but pressure. No soundtrack, no voice. Only the tension of the surface being broken.

Emotional Core

One word. One that costs something to say. What remains is a mark. And silence.

Technical Approach

Created entirely in CGI using Houdini, Nuke, and DaVinci Resolve. No filmed footage, no generative AI. Every frame crafted by hand.
