EN IT
Works About Contact

Paolo Ricaldone

Emotions are the compass, the fuel, and the engine of my work.
When something truly resonates, for better or worse, it is the stomach that reacts first — before the mind, before words.
We live surrounded by means of communication, yet deep human connection continues to fail.
Emotions — what truly animates us — rarely pass through directly: they are filtered, decoded, adjusted.
They never arrive intact.
That is why I seek visual forms for emotional territories.
Not to explain them, but to bring them closer to a more immediate passage.
I look for works that may become a bridge between inner worlds, meant to be felt before they are understood.
Not only to recognize oneself in them. Also to declare oneself through them.
My 1/1 video works are entirely handcrafted in digital form, with care. No generative AI.
Every element — light, matter, rhythm, composition — exists to sustain the emotional tension of the work, never to decorate it.
